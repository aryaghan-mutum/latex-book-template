When I was down beside the sea \\*
A wooden spade they gave to me \\*
To dig the sandy shore. \\*
My holes were empty like a cup. \\*
In every hole the sea came up \\*
Till it could come no more. \\*
\vspace{3mm}

\centerline{\textit{--- 
At the Sea-side
by Robert Louis Stevenson)}} 

\end{center}
